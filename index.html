Fruit Ninja Memory Game!

HOW TO PLAY: Press start to see & hear the first fruit clue. Press the matching button to repeat the clue and cut the fruit!
The sequence of clues increases each turn. After the clues play, you have 5 seconds to repeat it back.
The clues speed up as you progress, and you have 3 lives until you lose the game.

Countdown Timer: 5 seconds left!

Lives: 3
